1. Final Report

Components:

[] project title,

[] students' names,

[] the objective of the project, 

[] the motivation for the project,

[] background needed to understand the problem and the outcomes,

[] the methodology (plan) followed for achieving the proposed objective, 
[changes from the project plan in the project proposal are possible with justification]

[] literature review, discussing related works and their connection to this project,
project-specific and plan-specific outcomes, such as
  [] model specification,
  [] problem formulation and statement,
  [] proposed algorithm(s), solution, and studies, and
  [] results from theoretical analysis, simulations, and experiments,

[] discussion of the outcomes and the insights obtained from them, and

[] conclusion and future work (i.e., if someone wants to continue this project, what steps you recommend, or if they want to build upon this project, what directions you think are promising).

Formatting

[] 8 pages

[] Times New Roman, size <=11

[] Justified format

[] Standard citation format,
   references can go over 8 pages


[] PDF
